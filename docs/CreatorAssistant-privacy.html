Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Data Collection

CreatorAssistant runs locally on your device. Video processing and clip extraction are performed entirely on your computer. We do not collect, store, or transmit your video content or personal data.

2. Third-Party Services

When you use the YouTube or TikTok upload features, you authenticate directly with those platforms. OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device. CreatorAssistant does not have access to your platform credentials beyond what is required for uploads.

3. Local Storage

CreatorAssistant stores configuration and authentication tokens locally in the application folder. These files remain on your device and are not shared.

4. No Analytics

CreatorAssistant does not include analytics, tracking, or data-collection code.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the tool's maintainer.
