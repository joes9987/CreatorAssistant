Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

CreatorAssistant is a desktop application. All video processing — highlight detection, clip extraction, and format conversion — runs entirely on your device. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your video content.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

CreatorAssistant does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, usage analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or video content on remote servers. There is no account system and no sign-up required to use the application itself.

3. Third-Party Platform Authentication

When you use the upload features, you authenticate directly with YouTube (Google), TikTok, or Instagram (Meta) through their official OAuth flows. CreatorAssistant receives an access token from these platforms so it can upload clips on your behalf. These tokens are stored locally on your device in the application folder and are never sent to us or any third party.

The only data sent to third-party platforms is the video file you choose to upload, along with metadata you configure (title, description, tags). This data is transmitted directly from your device to the platform's API.

4. Riot Games API

The optional game events logger connects to Riot Games' local Live Client Data API (localhost port 2999) to read in-game kill events during an active League of Legends match. This data never leaves your device — it is written to a local JSON file used for clip timing.

5. Local Storage

CreatorAssistant stores the following files locally in the application folder:

  • Configuration (config.yaml) — your settings and API keys
  • OAuth tokens (youtube_token.json, tiktok_token.json, instagram_token.json) — platform access tokens
  • Event logs (eventlogs/) — kill timestamps from gameplay sessions
  • GUI preferences (gui_settings.json) — default folder path

These files remain on your device and are not shared with anyone.

6. Data Deletion

To remove all data, delete the application folder. To revoke platform access, visit the connected apps settings on Google, TikTok, or Instagram.

7. Children's Privacy

CreatorAssistant is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at creatorassistant.app@gmail.com.
