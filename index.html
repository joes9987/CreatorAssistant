CreatorAssistant

League of Legends highlight clipping tool for YouTube Shorts and TikTok

About

CreatorAssistant is a desktop application that helps League of Legends content creators extract and publish gameplay highlights. It runs locally on your computer—no cloud processing or data collection.

Record your games with OBS, run CreatorAssistant to detect exciting moments (kills, team fights), and upload clips directly to YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

How It Works

  • Record — Use OBS to record your League games.
  • Detect — CreatorAssistant finds highlight moments using in-game kill data (Riot API) or AI (audio + motion).
  • Extract — Cuts clips in 9:16 vertical format, ready for Shorts and Reels.
  • Upload — Publishes clips to YouTube and TikTok with one-click OAuth.

Integrations

YouTube Shorts — OAuth 2.0 login. Clips upload to your channel with custom titles and tags.

TikTok — Login Kit (OAuth) lets users authorize CreatorAssistant to post clips to their TikTok account. Users sign in via browser, grant access, and the app uploads clips on their behalf. All processing happens locally; only the final video is sent to TikTok.

Privacy & Security

CreatorAssistant processes videos entirely on your device. OAuth tokens for YouTube and TikTok are stored locally and never shared. No analytics, tracking, or data collection.
