CreatorAssistant

Clip, convert, and publish gameplay highlights — built for creators

What It Does

CreatorAssistant is a free desktop tool for content creators. It can automatically detect highlights in League of Legends recordings and extract vertical clips — or you can skip clipping entirely and use it to upload any video straight to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels.

League creators get the full workflow: record with OBS, let CreatorAssistant find kills and team fights, and publish 9:16 clips in one step. Other creators can use it as a simple multi-platform uploader.

How It Works

  • Record — Capture your League games with OBS (or any recorder).
  • Detect — CreatorAssistant identifies highlight moments using live kill data from Riot's API or AI-based audio and motion analysis.
  • Extract — Clips are cut and converted to 9:16 vertical format, optimized for short-form platforms.
  • Publish — Upload clips to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels with one-click OAuth. You stay in control of what gets posted.
  • Direct upload — Already have clips ready? Skip detection and extraction — just pick your files and upload to all platforms at once.

Platform Integrations

YouTube Shorts — Sign in with Google OAuth. Clips upload directly to your channel with custom titles, descriptions, and tags.

TikTok — CreatorAssistant uses TikTok's Login Kit so you can authorize the app to post clips to your account. You sign in via browser, grant access, and the app uploads on your behalf. All video processing happens locally — only the final clip is sent to TikTok.

Instagram Reels — Connect your Instagram account through Meta's OAuth flow. Clips are uploaded as Reels with configurable captions.

Who It's For

CreatorAssistant is built primarily for League of Legends creators, but any content creator can use it. The highlight detection and game event logging are League-specific, while the upload workflow works with any video file. Whether you're a streamer building a highlights channel, a casual player sharing plays, or a creator who just needs a quick way to post to multiple platforms — this tool is for you.

Privacy & Security

All video processing runs locally on your device. OAuth tokens for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram are stored on your machine and never leave it. CreatorAssistant does not collect analytics, telemetry, or personal data.
