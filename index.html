CreatorAssistant

Automatic highlight clipping for League of Legends.
Record, detect, extract, upload.

About

CreatorAssistant is a desktop tool that helps League of Legends content creators extract and publish gameplay highlights. It runs locally on your computer — no cloud processing or data collection.

Record your games with OBS, run CreatorAssistant to detect exciting moments (kills, team fights), and upload clips directly to YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

How It Works

1 Record

Use OBS to record your League games as you play.

2 Detect

CreatorAssistant finds highlights using kill data or AI analysis.

3 Extract

Clips are cut in 9:16 vertical format, ready for Shorts and Reels.

4 Upload

Publish clips to YouTube and TikTok with one-click OAuth.

Integrations

YouTube Shorts

OAuth 2.0 login. Clips upload to your channel with custom titles and tags.

TikTok

Login Kit (OAuth) lets you authorize CreatorAssistant to post clips to your TikTok account. All processing happens locally.

Privacy & Security

CreatorAssistant processes videos entirely on your device. OAuth tokens for YouTube and TikTok are stored locally and never shared. No analytics, tracking, or data collection.
